Accommodation

The hostel consists of four rooms: three twin rooms and one four-bedded room. There is one bathroom with a toilet and a shower, a separate shower room, and an additional toilet.

Our common areas include a living room with a comfortable seating area and TV; as well as a fully-equipped kitchen with a stove, oven, microwave, dishwasher, electric kettle, and coffee maker. Next to the kitchen and lounge, there is a glass veranda with several dining tables with access to a large outdoor patio.

We also have three separate cottages for rent.

Breakfast is self-service with full access to the kitchen. Please let us know if you have any special dietary requests or allergies in advance. Frozen pies and ready-made meals are normally available in the freezer. You are welcome to store your own food in the refrigerator or freezer.

Transport: As we are located a few kilometers outside of Växjö, we offer transport to/from the city for a small fee by arrangement in advance, for example pick-up or drop-off at the station or the university. Please note that this offer is subject to availability.
