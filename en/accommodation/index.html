Accommodation

The hostel part contains four rooms; three 2-bed rooms and one 4-bed room. There is one toilet with a shower, one separate shower room, and an additional toilet.

Common areas include a lounge with sofa seating and TV, plus TVs in the rooms. The fully equipped kitchen contains a stove, oven, microwave, dishwasher, electric kettle, and coffee maker. A glass veranda with dining tables connects the kitchen and lounge, opening onto a large outdoor patio.

Timmerhuset (The Timber House) is a separate building with a sleeping loft, five beds, a TV corner, lounge, kitchenette, toilet, shower, and a stone fireplace.

Breakfast is self-service; please inform staff of any dietary preferences and allergies in advance. Frozen pies and ready meals are available. Personal food storage in the refrigerator is provided.

Transport to/from the city centre can be arranged at mileage rates upon arrangement.
