Getting here

Address: Skir Nybygget 1, 355 91 Växjö

GPS: 56.828152, 14.855422

Directions from Växjö

Take Route 27 toward Karlskrona. At the last roundabout (Fagrabäcksrondellen), continue on Route 27 toward Karlskrona for about 6 kilometers. Turn right toward Kulladal (directly after a bus stop). Follow the road for about 1 kilometer to reach the farm – two yellow houses, a barn, and several red buildings.

Directions from the south (Route 27)

Heading toward Växjö, 2 kilometers after Bramstorp (about 1.5 km from Växjö), take a left toward Kulladal (there will be a red barn on the right side of the road). Follow the road for about 1 kilometer to reach the farm – two yellow houses, a barn, and several red buildings.
